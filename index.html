About Little Treat

Welcome to Little Treat, where passion meets perfection in every chocolate creation. Located in the vibrant city of Ahmedabad, we specialize in handcrafted chocolates and artisanal confections that bring joy to every moment.

Our journey began with a simple dream - to create chocolates that not only taste extraordinary but also tell a story of craftsmanship, quality, and love. Every piece is carefully made using the finest ingredients sourced from around the world.

Pre-Orders & Gift Hampers: We work on a pre-order basis to ensure maximum freshness and quality. Looking for the perfect gift? Our beautiful gift hampers are customized for birthdays, anniversaries, festivals, and corporate events. Each hamper is thoughtfully curated and elegantly packaged.

Meet Our Founders

Foram & Parth - The creative minds behind Little Treat. With years of experience in confectionery arts and a shared passion for innovation, they bring unique flavors and artistic designs that make every treat a memorable experience.
